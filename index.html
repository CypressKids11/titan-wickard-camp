SCP Foundation

The SCP Foundation is a secretive organization that handles Anomalous things, beings, and entitys. It does this in a secretive way. Once it finds a Anomalous thing, being, or entity it lables it as a SCP with a number beind it (Ex.SCP-049). The SCP's are then put into categories. These are Safe,Eulicd,Keter,Thaumiel,Apollyon,Archon, and Neutralized(Ex.SCP-049,Eulicd).The name means Secure, Contain, Protect
